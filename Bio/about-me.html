Clubs I'm apart of!

Taekwondo Club: Fall 2025-Present

Role: Officer | Treasurer

In the Fall 2025 semester I decided to join the Taekwondo Club here at UNCC after previously starting it in the summer at King Tiger Taekwondo. This club meets twice a week at the UNCC gym that is welcome to new or more Experienced members that are interested in it. The club was very beginner friendly as I was able to learn all of the basic white belt techninques and begin to slowly train into the more advanced kicks. The club also prioritizes sparring at the end where we get to practice all the techniques in a real and live fight rather than just practicing on our own. Through the club I have learned so much and am on my way to the Yellow belt within just one semester. The overall Experience has been amazing as I have not omly learned the traditional techniques of Taekwondo but also how to spare and defend myself with those same techniques. I also do plan to compete in competitions as well as my coach recommended me for it!

Criminal Justice Association: Fall 2025-Present

Role: Member

Also in my Fall 2025 semester I joined the Criminal Justice Association after deciding to minor in Criminal Justice prior. This club offered many incredible Experiences whether it be from the more casual get together chapter meetings to the guest speakers where many interesting people from the field came in to talk to us. We got to meet several police officers from different counties who all offered new and unique stories and perspectives of the criminal justice field. My favorite guest speaker from one of the club meetings was an officer who did a lot of work in digital forensics and gave some great resources on how to get started in learning computer forensics
